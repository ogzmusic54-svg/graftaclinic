# Grafta Clinic Design Document

## 1. Brand Identity & Visual Style
Inspired by the Esteworld website, Grafta Clinic will maintain a premium, trustworthy, and medical-grade aesthetic. The design should feel professional, clean, and modern, using high-quality imagery and a sophisticated color palette.

### Color Palette
- **Primary:** Deep Slate Blue (#1A2B48) - For trust and professionalism.
- **Secondary/Accent:** Gold or Soft Bronze (#C5A059) - To signify premium service and "aesthetic" excellence.
- **Background:** Off-White/Light Gray (#F8F9FA) - For a clean, clinical feel.
- **Text:** Dark Gray (#333333) for readability.

### Typography
- **Headings:** Serif font (e.g., Playfair Display) for a luxurious and established feel.
- **Body:** Sans-serif font (e.g., Montserrat or Inter) for clarity and modernity.

## 2. Information Architecture
The website will be organized into clear categories based on the requested services:

### Navigation Structure
- **Home**
- **Hair Restoration**
    - Hair Transplant (Saç Ekimi)
    - Beard Transplant (Sakal Ekimi)
    - Eyebrow Transplant (Kaş Ekimi)
- **Treatments**
    - Alopecia Treatment (Saç Kıran Tedavisi)
    - PRP Therapy
    - Mesotherapy (Mezoterapi)
- **Medical Aesthetics**
    - Fillers & Botox (Dolgu & Botox)
    - Youth Vaccine (Gençlik Aşısı)
- **Plastic Surgery (Estetik)**
    - Rhinoplasty (Burun Estetiği)
    - Breast Aesthetics (Göğüs Estetiği)
- **Contact/Appointment**

## 3. Design Components & Dynamics
- **Hero Section:** Large, high-quality images of satisfied patients and professional clinical environment. Clear Call-to-Action (CTA) for "Free Consultation".
- **Service Cards:** Clean grids with subtle hover effects. Use professional photography for each service category.
- **Medical Team:** A dedicated section showing the expert doctors, emphasizing trust.
- **Patient Testimonials/Results:** "Before & After" sections (where compliant) and video testimonials.
- **Trust Signals:** Logos of certifications, global reach map, and hospital stats (as seen in the reference).
- **Sticky Header:** For easy navigation with a prominent "Book Appointment" button.
- **Detailed Footer:** Comprehensive list of services and contact information.

## 4. Specific Page Requirements
- **Homepage:** A comprehensive overview of all services with quick links to each department.
- **Service Detail Pages:** In-depth explanation of the procedure, benefits, and FAQ for each service like "Saç Ekimi" or "Burun Estetiği".
